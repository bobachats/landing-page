Our origin story

Jeff Zhang launched SpeakUp Venture in March 2021 after the "Atlanta Spa Shooting". He recruited his first group of interns including Chendan.



To learn about everyone’s stories, the team started internal “Boba Chats”.



After seeing how intimate the team became, Jeff decided to pivot to Boba chats to create a space for genuine conversations where the community understands and supports each other.



Boba Chats officially launched in June 2021.



The rest is still unwritten...
